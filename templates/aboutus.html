About Us

GetHome is a community based on connections.A community established on the year 2020 when three friends thought about the idea of connecting the owner of the house and the tenant in India. It has started with 3 hosts and 5 tenants and now it has grown upto 5,000 hosts and 40,000+ tenants. At GetHome,we work in an environment where every employee works as it's their resposibility to serve the people by meeting their maximum needs with less effort.
